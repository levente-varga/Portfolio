About

I joined the robotics student group at the Óbudai Árpád Secondary Grammar School in Budapest, Hungary.

It combined two of my main interests: programming and LEGO.

I was the main robot designer, but I also contributed to programming.

Participated in First Lego League (FLL) team competitions in 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2017.

Participated in World Robot Olympiad (WRO) team competitions in 2016, 2017 and 2018.

Used Lego NXT and Lego EV3 models.

Programmed with Lego Mindstorms Home Edition.

Period: Sep 2013 - June 2018

WRO 2018
8 June - 9 June, 2018

By far the most complex robot I ever designed.

It had a vertically moving arm with a color sensor, serving four different purposes: following lines, detecting colors in front of it, collecting and storing 2x2 cubes, and lifting up objects by their hoops.

It also had an inner storage between its wheels that could hold all three objects of one kind.
