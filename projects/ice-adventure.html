Assets

Sprites made by me in Gimp used in the game.

The characters can be easily distinguished by the shape of their head alone (even while in water), making the game more accessible.

Rules

The goal is to find three flare gun parts, then assemble and fire it.

If any player dies, the game ends.

All players start on the top-left ice floe.

Some floes contain items under layers of snow. To get these, players have to dig.

Blizzards arrive at random intervals, covering tiles in snow and damaging players who are not standing on a tile with shelter (tent or igloo).

Polar bears serve as an additional threat, killing anyone who gets close.

Ice floes have a maximum load capacity (number of players), breaking if overloaded. When a player falls into water, others have one turn to rescue them.
