Other thesis topics

A thorough comparison between Unity's built-in shaders of the High Definition Render Pipeline was also part of the thesis. This included both visual and performance comparisons in suitable test scenes.

Some of those works can be found below.

Global illumination

Comparison between disabled (left), screen-space (middle) and ray-traced (right) global illumination.

A directional light was used to light the scene enclosed between two walls.

The screen-space shader produced a strange blue artifact below the white ball, even though the scene did not contain any object of that color.

Subsurface scattering

Comparison between disabled (left), screen space (middle) and ray-traced (right) subsurface scattering.

The monkey head was lit from behind. The screen-space shader produced a surprisingly good result.

Shadows

Comparison between disabled (left), screen space (middle) and ray-traced (right) shadows.

This simple scene was perfect for demonstrating the details in the penumbra.
