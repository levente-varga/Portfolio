About

Disclaimer: I had a minor role in the development process as I was new to GDScript, and most of the code was carried over from a team member's previous projects

3-person group project as part of the Interactive and Linear Storytelling with Digital Media course at the University of Gothenburg

Inspired by The Stanley Parable and backrooms-like games

Developed with Godot .NET in GDScript

Features a gameplay loop with a narrator

Interactable objects, custom music, voice lines, and subtitles.

Development period: Nov 2023 - Dec 2023

Status: Complete
