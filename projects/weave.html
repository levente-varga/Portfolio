About

University group project for the Game Development Project course

The theme was teamwork

We came up with a combination of Tron and Snake with a co-op twist

Developed with Godot .NET in C#

Player number is virtually unlimited

Features a balanced scoring system that is independent of the number of player

Animations and custom music

Presented at an exhibition in Gothenburg

Developed between: Sept 2023 - Nov 2023

Game over

Players control a line that is constantly moving and gradually accelerating

Each player has to reach a pickup of their corresponding color

Doing so grants score, resets the game field and spawns new pickups and obstacles

Gameplay

Upon collision with an obstacle or a line, the game ends

The score was saved to our locally hosted server and was shown during the exhibition
