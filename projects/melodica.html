About

Giving rhythm games another go after Rhythm Ninja, this time in Godot

An experimentation to learn the engine

Developed with Godot .NET in C#

Features a tutorial level and a menu

Start of development: Feb 2024

Status: On hold

Gameplay

The player has to press the shown button when the note reaches the line

Accuracy is measured and graded

The smooth note movement is a combination of delta time and the audio player's playhead position
